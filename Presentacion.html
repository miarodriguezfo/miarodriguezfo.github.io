Iron Man (2008)

General information

The film Iron Man is based on the comics of Marvel written by Stan Lee. The film was directed by Jon Favreau who acts in the movie too. The main role was played by Robert Downey Jr as Tony Stark.

The film was nominated in two categories in the Academy awards:

  • -Best visual effects

  • -Best sound editing

Was cataloged on the top ten of movies of the year in 2008 by the American Film Institute

The film is about an engineer and billionaire businessman that runs Stark Industries a technology company dedicated to the weapon development. In a demostration of a missile in Middle East Tony Stark is attacked by a terrorist group, suffers serious injuries and is kidnapped.

There is a who scientist saves his life with a reactor in his chest that avoids the shrapnel hurt his heart. The kidnappers obligate to Stark to build a missile but with the materials he build a better reactor and a suit that protects him from the weapons and with this suit he gets to escape.

When he returns, he announces that Stark Industries will no longer manufacture weapons. Stark begins to improve this new technology and build a new suit with new features but Obadiah a executive in Stark Industries wants the suit to convert it in a new weapon and sell it to differents armies.

While Tony works on his own suit with the reactor in his chest discover the need of a material that support the low temperature when leaves the atmosphere and Obadiah build his own version of the suit with an old reactor that was in Stark Industries.

The film ends with a fight between Tony and Obadiah, each one in his suit but Stark knows that the material of the Obadiah’s Suit doesn’t support the low temperature and carry the fight out of the atmosphere and there he beats to Obadiah. Finally in a press conference Tony reveals to the world that he is Iron Man..

Locations

The film is set in afghanistan and Malibu during the 2010s, it was filmed on location in Alabama Hills at Lone Pine on Route 395, central California and Stark Industry’s air field is Edwards Air Force Base.
